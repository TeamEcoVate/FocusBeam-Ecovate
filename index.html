Focus with Purpose

FocusBeam is designed to promote concentration and mindfulness during study sessions. Built with a Light Dependent Resistor (LDR), it senses your phone placement and activates the LED lighting, signaling the start of your focus timer.

A built-in OLED screen shows your study duration, and data is sent to the connected web interface for tracking progress. With its eco-friendly frame made from recycled materials, FocusBeam represents the future of smart, sustainable studying.
