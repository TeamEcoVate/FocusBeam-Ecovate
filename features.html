FocusBeam

Smart, eco-friendly study lamp for distraction-free productivity.

What is FocusBeam

FocusBeam is a smart study lamp developed by EcoVate to help students and individuals stay focused while studying. It automatically detects phone placement using a light-dependent resistor (LDR) and turns on the lamp to signal a distraction-free study session.

The lamp integrates an OLED display with a built-in timer to track study periods, adjustable RGB lighting for optimal concentration, and a USB charging port to safely charge devices while keeping them out of sight. Data from each session can be displayed on a connected website to monitor study habits.
